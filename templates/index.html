MathNLG

Proving the concept of a Natural Language Generator capable of generating mathematical definitions and proofs.

Motivation of that idea is improving quality of scientific literature. It is not easy to collaborate on writing a book, a lot of redundant work is being done in the process, which could be automatize by a machine.

Humans have limited patience and focus, everyone has own individual style. All this results with books which are not accurate enough, skip relevant steps and definitions, which is decreasing quality of end result.
